# 2026-04-05 — Replace Gumbel-Top-K with Greedy Top-K in Node RL

## What Changed

- `rl/model.py`: `select()` now always uses deterministic Top-K selection. Gumbel noise sampling code removed. `log_prob` is always computed and returned (never `None`).
- `rl/train.py`: Removed `explore_during_training` parameter from `run_training_node_rl()`. Training rollouts now use greedy Top-K, same as evaluation.

## Why

Ablation experiment showed that **Greedy Top-K consistently outperforms Gumbel-Top-K** in both tested scenarios. Gumbel exploration noise hurts performance because:

1. With a tight daily vaccine budget (K=10), wasting even 1 dose on a suboptimal node causes extra infections/deaths.
2. The stochastic epidemic environment already provides natural diversity across episodes.
3. PPO parameter updates implicitly shift the scoring function, providing sufficient exploration without explicit noise.

## Experimental Evidence

Ablation: `experiments/ablation_gumbel_topk.py` (300 episodes training, 10 eval runs)

| Scenario | OC-Guided | Node RL (Gumbel) | Node RL (Greedy) |
|----------|-----------|------------------|------------------|
| baseline (dY=0.27) | 25.5 +/- 3.7 | 29.0 +/- 4.3 | **26.1 +/- 4.0** |
| moderate (dY=0.40) | 35.3 +/- 6.8 | 43.4 +/- 6.2 | **38.6 +/- 6.8** |

Greedy is better by ~3 deaths (baseline) and ~5 deaths (moderate).
